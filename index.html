All about Squirtle

Base Stats

Squirtle is a water type pokemon that is a first generation starter. Squirtle starts off with 44 HP, 48 attack, 65 defense and 43 speed.

Evolutions

Here is a list of Squirtles evolutions, these do not include any EX or Vstar versions;

  • Basic- Squirtle
  • Stage 1- Wartortle
  • Stage 2- Blastoise

Attacks

Squirtle has many different attacks he can do, but you start off with Tackle, a full body charge attack, Tail Whip, a move that lowers opponents defense and Water Gun which shoots water at the opponent.

All about Bulbasaur

Base Stats

Bulbasaur is a grass/poison type pokemon that is also a first generation starter. Bulbasaur starts off with 45 HP, 49 attack, 49 defense and 45 speed.

Evolutions

Here is the list of Bulbasaurs evolutions, these also do not include any EX or Vstar versions;

  • Basic- Bulbasaur
  • Stage 1- Ivysaur
  • Stage 2- Venusaur

Attacks

Bulbasaurs beginning attacks are Growl, which lowers opponents attack power, Tackle, which is a full body charge attack and Vine Whip, which whips the opponents with Bulbasaurs vines.

All about Charmander

Charmander is a fire type pokemon that was introduced as a first generation starter. Charmander starts off with 39 HP, 52 attack, 43 defense and 65 speed.

Evolutions

Here is the list of Charmanders evolutions without including EX and Vstar versions;

  • Basic- Charmander
  • Stage 1 - Charmeleon
  • Stage 2- Charizard

Attacks

Charmanders beginning attacks are Growl, which lowers opponents attack power, Scratch, which scratches the opponent with claws and Ember which is an attack that may cause the opponent to be burned.
